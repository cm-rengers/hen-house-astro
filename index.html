The house

Quiet, generous, and just enough.

The Hen House is a reimagined farm building, designed for unhurried weekends and small celebrations. Thoughtful textures, considered light, and a calm palette make space for long conversations, deep sleeps, and early walks.

Tucked just off a quiet lane near Penrith, you’re ten minutes from the M6 but wrapped in fields, hedgerows, and big skies. Wake to birdsong, walk out onto the terrace with coffee, and let your shoulders drop.

Location

Quietly placed, easily reached.

The Hen House sits just outside a small Cumbrian village, ten minutes from the M6 at Penrith. You’re close to Ullswater, the Eden Valley, and the Yorkshire Dales, with farm shops, walks, and swimming spots within easy reach.

  • 10 minutes from the M6, yet wrapped in open countryside.
  • 20 minutes to Ullswater and lake shore walks.
  • Local pub, bakery, and farm shop a short drive away.
